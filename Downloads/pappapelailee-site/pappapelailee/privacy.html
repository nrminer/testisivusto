Privacy policy

Last updated: April 2026

Plain-language explanation of what data PappaPelailee.com collects, what it does with it, and what rights you have over it.

Who is responsible for your data

PappaPelailee.com is operated by an individual publisher based in Finland. For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact details are on the About page.

What data we collect and why

Raffle and giveaway entries

When you submit an entry to a community giveaway or raffle on this site, we collect:

  • Your Discord username — so the winner can be contacted via Discord.
  • Your entry data — depending on the raffle type, this is either a set of roulette numbers you picked, an end-session balance figure you provide, or a game name.
  • A timestamp of when you submitted.

This data is stored in a cloud database (Vercel KV / Upstash Redis) accessible only to the site operator. It is used solely to run the raffle, verify entries, and contact the winner. No financial information is collected. The balance figure you enter is self-reported by you for competition purposes and is not linked to any payment account.

Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6): Performance of a contract — you voluntarily enter a giveaway and provide information as a condition of participation.

Retention: Raffle entries are kept until the associated raffle is closed and a winner has been drawn, after which they may be cleared. Entries are never sold or shared with third parties.

Server and access logs

The site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel automatically records standard server access logs including your IP address, browser user-agent, and the pages you request. These logs exist for security and infrastructure purposes and are governed by Vercel's privacy policy. This site does not read or process those logs.

What we do not collect

  • We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any behavioural tracking.
  • We do not set cookies from this domain.
  • We do not collect email addresses.
  • We do not collect real names, phone numbers, or any identity document.
  • We do not run any translation or localisation scripts from third-party CDNs.

Third-party services

Google Fonts

The site loads typefaces (Fraunces, IBM Plex Sans, IBM Plex Mono) from Google Fonts. When your browser requests these fonts, Google's servers receive your IP address and the referring page URL. Google states that font requests are not associated with Google account activity and are deleted after a short period. See: Google Fonts privacy FAQ.

Affiliate links and casino sites

When you click a "Visit casino →" link, you are taken to an external casino website. That casino has its own privacy policy, its own cookie practices, and is an entirely separate data controller. This site has no visibility into what happens on the casino's side after you click. Always read the casino's terms before registering. See our affiliate disclosure for more on how affiliate links work.

Your rights under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights in relation to personal data this site holds about you:

  • Access — You can ask what data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — You can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — You can ask us to delete your raffle entry data.
  • Restriction — You can ask us to stop using your data while a dispute is resolved.
  • Portability — You can ask for a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the details on the About page. We aim to respond within 30 days. If you are unhappy with our response, you can lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi).

Children

This site reviews online gambling products and is intended for adults aged 18 and over only. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted a raffle entry, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to how we handle your data, we will update this page and change the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the site after a change is published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy question, data request, or concern, use the contact details on the About page.
